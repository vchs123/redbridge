Your Roadmap to
Employment

A structured 4-step journey from graduation to your first professional role.

Month 1

Skill Gap Analysis

We analyze your university transcript and current skills against market requirements. We identify exactly what tools (Xero, AWS, Revit) you are missing.

Month 2

Technical Training

Intensive bootcamp. You stop learning theory and start learning the actual software used in Australian firms. 4 weeks of hands-on practice.

Month 3-5

Real Project Experience

The Core Phase. You join a team to deliver a real project for a local client. 400+ hours of loggable work experience to satisfy skill assessments.

Month 6

Placement & Reference

We build your portfolio, rewrite your CV with your new experience, provide a professional reference, and connect you with our hiring network.
