Points Strategy by Occupation

The 189 visa is competitive. Here is how we maximize your score.

Accounting & Finance

For Accountants, Auditors, and Finance Managers. This occupation group requires high points due to high competition.

  • Critical Factor: Superior English + PY

The Application Lifecycle

From points calculation to PR grant.

01
Step 1

Eligibility Audit

We analyze your age, english, study, and partner factors to calculate your raw score. If you are under 65 points, we stop here.

02
Step 2

Skills Assessment

The most critical technical step. We lodge your assessment with the relevant authority (ACS, CPA, EA, VETASSESS) to prove your occupation.

03
Step 3

Points Maximization

While waiting for the assessment, we enroll you in PY or NAATI courses and guide your English test prep to squeeze out every possible point.

04
Step 4

EOI & Invitation

We lodge your Expression of Interest (EOI) with SkillSelect. Once invited, we have 60 days to lodge your final PR application.
