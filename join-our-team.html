Marketing Specialist Intern

Docklands, VIC Part-Time / Full-Time Hybrid Setup
Apply Now

RedBridge Consulting is looking for a creative, proactive, and driven Marketing Specialist Intern to join our Docklands team. You will gain hands-on experience in digital marketing, social media management, and brand strategy, directly contributing to campaigns that help international talents secure their future in Australia.

What You Will Do

  • Manage and grow our social media presence across platforms like Instagram, TikTok, and XiaoHongShu (RedNote).
  • Assist in creating compelling visual and written content (graphics, short-form videos, copy).
  • Conduct market research to identify trends in the Australian migration and employment sectors.
  • Monitor campaign analytics and help optimize digital ad performance.
  • Collaborate with the sales and consulting teams to align marketing efforts with business goals.

Who You Are

  • Currently pursuing or recently graduated with a degree in Marketing, Communications, or a related field.
  • Strong understanding of social media algorithms and trends, particularly on Instagram and XiaoHongShu.
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills (Bilingual proficiency in English & Mandarin is highly regarded).
  • Basic design skills using tools like Canva, Figma, or Adobe Creative Suite.
  • A self-starter who thrives in a fast-paced, collaborative environment.

What We Offer

  • Real, loggable commercial experience with a highly professional team.
  • Mentorship from experienced industry leaders.
  • Potential pathway to a permanent role based on performance.
  • Modern office in Docklands with flexible hybrid working arrangements.
